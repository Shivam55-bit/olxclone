# Sellbuytm - Netlify Deployment Instructions

This folder contains static HTML pages for Privacy Policy and Account Deletion that can be deployed to Netlify.

## Files Included

- `index.html` - Home page with links to all pages
- `privacy-policy.html` - Detailed privacy policy page
- `delete-account.html` - Account deletion instructions and information
- `styles.css` - Shared CSS styling for all pages

## How to Deploy to Netlify

### Method 1: Drag and Drop (Easiest)

1. Go to [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)
2. Sign in or create a free account
3. Click on "Sites" in the top navigation
4. Drag and drop the entire `netlify-pages` folder onto the deployment area
5. Wait for deployment to complete (usually 30-60 seconds)
6. Your site will be live with a random Netlify URL (e.g., `random-name-123.netlify.app`)

### Method 2: Manual Upload

1. Go to [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)
2. Sign in or create a free account
3. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
4. Drag and drop the entire `netlify-pages` folder
5. Wait for deployment
6. Your site will be live!

## Customization

Before deploying, you may want to customize:

- **Contact Information**: Update email addresses and phone numbers in all HTML files
- **Company Name**: The app name is set to "Sellbuytm"
- **Styling**: Modify `styles.css` to match your brand colors
- **Content**: Update privacy policy details and deletion procedures to match your actual practices

## After Deployment

Once deployed, you'll get a URL like: `https://your-site-name.netlify.app`

You can:
- Use this URL in your mobile app
- Add a custom domain (in Netlify settings)
- Enable HTTPS (automatic with Netlify)
- View deployment logs and analytics
